CORRECT FRONTEND COMMANDS
=========================

Use PowerShell:

cd "C:\Projects\IOT\Internet of Things - IoT-Based Air Quality & Pollution Monitoring Dashboard\air_quality_frontend"
.\run_frontend.bat

If PowerShell has issue, use:

cmd /c run_frontend.bat

Open:

http://127.0.0.1:5500

Backend must be running separately at:

http://127.0.0.1:8000

Correct order:

1) Backend window:
cd "C:\Projects\IOT\Internet of Things - IoT-Based Air Quality & Pollution Monitoring Dashboard\air_quality_backend"
.\run_backend.bat

2) Frontend window:
cd "C:\Projects\IOT\Internet of Things - IoT-Based Air Quality & Pollution Monitoring Dashboard\air_quality_frontend"
.\run_frontend.bat

Do not close either window while using the dashboard.
